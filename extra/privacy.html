Section A: Our Collection, Use, and Disclosure of Personal Information

The Categories of Personal Information We Collect

  • Personal Details: Information such as your email address, name, phone number, and postal address.
  • Payment Details: Payment information including payment history and billing address.
  • Account/Profile Information: Data linked to your Notflix account, such as profile settings, watchlist, and preferences.
  • Usage Information: Interaction details like playback events, search queries, and usage duration.
  • Advertising Information: For ad-supported plans, data about advertisements viewed or interacted with.
  • Device and Network Information: Details about devices and networks used to access Notflix, such as device IDs and IP addresses.
